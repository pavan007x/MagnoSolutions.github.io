Software Development

Perhaps your business has grown and you are struggling to get everything done with the systems which have worked fine up to now? One solution is to employ more staff. A more cost-effective solution would be to build a database application.

Perhaps you need better access to information about your business? You know that this information can be obtained from your current systems, but it's a time consuming manual process. A properly designed database application could produce this information for you at the click of a button.

Perhaps you have a problem with your current system? The person who developed it has moved on, and you'd like to make changes, but you're not sure how to. Or perhaps you've upgraded your PCs or database software and find you have error messages, or worse still that you've lost your database passwords and can't get in?

Online Marketing

Once your website has been designed and developed, you can no longer just leave it and expect your number of leads to increase and to get to the top of Google. Instead, you have to market your website and your online content much like you would market a new product.

At Magno Solutions, we strive to not only create a high quality website that fits your needs, but keep it 'working' for your company or organization. The purpose of online marketing is to increase traffic to your website, increase the number of conversions from visitor to lead and increase brand awareness.

Online marketing is a very broad topic; however we will identify the solution that works best for you. Whether that's social media marketing, email campaigns, content marketing, SEO or any other suitable method to get your website in the right place on the internet and improve its performance.
