Magno AMS

Magno AMS (Academic Management System) is a total solution for school and college management system. AMS is a complete package of educational Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP). A range of modules of our ERP would facilitate you to manage your institution successfully. AMS helps in controlling the working and functionality of your institution. It also strengthens the bond between students, teachers, parents, management and the administration staff of your institution. The open ended and scalable architecture enables our ERP to be customized product according to your unique requirements. Concisely, AMS is a comprehensive and an extremely user friendly solution.

Magno CRM

Magno CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is all about managing the relationships you have with your customers. Magno CRM combines business processes, people, and technology to achieve this single goal: getting and keeping customers. It's an overall strategy to help you learn more about their behaviour so you can develop stronger, lasting relationships that will benefit both of you. Magno CRM involves many areas of your company, starting with sales and extending to other customer-facing areas like marketing and customer service. Magno offers a technology solution for all those areas and more. With Magno`s cloud computing platform, you can mind your customers and your budget at the same time.

Magno Realtor

Magno Realtor helps a company to facilitate between the Seller & the Buyer or Lessor & Lessee on identifying their requirements. In systems perspective the entire services of the company is classified as Selling / Buying / Renting Out / Required for Rent as business types and the properties are primarily categorized as Residential / Commercial & Land. The transactions start in getting an enquiry from a client on his requirement. The life cycle of the transaction is classified in to many stages to identify the progress and to fix the responsibility to complete that stage for an Individual.

Magno POS

Magno POS (Point of Sale) is retail outlet management software. The multi-branching facility helps you manage any number of outlets. All the screens of Magno POS are equipped with fast and user friendly search facility. The software mainly helps to maintain customer records, services and rates, generate service slips & service invoices, print invoices and reports. It also helps to generate sales reports, customer wise report, sales representative wise report.
