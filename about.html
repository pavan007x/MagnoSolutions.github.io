About Us

Magno Solutions Private Ltd. was formed to provide high quality and reliable solutions & services to its clients. Established in 2008, it aims to help companies provide end to end complex analysis solutions. Its proven expertise and multiple skills in different industry segments combine to realize technology-driven business transformation.

Magno Solutions has a young dynamic and highly motivated team, which is skill-rich and ready to take on. Its team is an excellent blend of technical engineers and business managers that have knowledge, international exposure and in-depth experience to ensure that Magno Solutions consistently meets its commitments.

In every project that it undertakes, Magno Solutions aims to exceed the expectations of its clients. Magno Solutions makes sure to have a good understanding of its client's business, their goals and objectives before designing a solution. This approach ensures that not only does Magno Solutions meet the client's technical requirements but also fulfills their business objectives.
